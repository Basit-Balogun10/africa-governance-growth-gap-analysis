## SLIDE 11: Policy Recommendations
**Headline:** "From Data to Decision: 5 Actionable Solutions"

**Recommendations:**

1. **üéØ Focus on Governance QUALITY, Not Quantity**
   - Stop measuring success by "Tax-to-GDP ratio"
   - Start measuring: "Growth per Dollar of Tax Collected"
   - Example: Ethiopia achieves 0.50% growth per % tax | Egypt achieves 0.27%

2. **üöÄ Study the East African Model**
   - Ethiopia, Rwanda, Tanzania: Private sector-led growth
   - Low tax burden = more capital for businesses
   - Infrastructure focus over bureaucracy expansion

3. **üîç Implement Fiscal Transparency Frameworks**
   - Publish: "Where does each tax dollar go?"
   - Create: Public dashboards tracking spending ‚Üí outcomes
   - Benchmark: Cross-country comparisons like this analysis

4. **ü§ù Coordinate Health & Education Investments**
   - Our correlation heatmap shows: 0.75 correlation between health-education spending
   - Countries prioritize BOTH or NEITHER
   - Recommendation: Bundle SDG 3 + SDG 4 policies

5. **üìä Build Debt Sustainability Early Warning Systems**
   - Egypt/Nigeria: Debt-to-GDP ratios spiraling
   - Trigger: When Debt Growth > GDP Growth for 3 consecutive years
   - Action: Automatic spending review + private sector incentives
